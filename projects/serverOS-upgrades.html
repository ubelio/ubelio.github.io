Implementation

  1. Audited all servers and categorized by OS version, role, and criticality
  2. Planned upgrade paths: in-place for low-risk, side-by-side for critical systems
  3. Backed up all systems and validated recovery procedures
  4. Upgraded domain controllers from Server 2016 to 2019 and 2022
  5. Executed phased upgrades across production servers with zero downtime
  6. Identified and decommissioned unused or legacy servers no longer required
  7. Validated services, roles, and data integrity post-upgrade
