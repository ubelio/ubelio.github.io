SC-401 Lab 10 – Configure Retention Policies

Implement retention policies to manage data lifecycle and ensure regulatory compliance across Microsoft 365 services.

Home Projects Scripts Home-Lab Config Labs Contact

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Learn Sandbox
  • Microsoft Purview Portal
  • Microsoft 365 Compliance Center

Steps Taken

  1. Signed into Microsoft Purview portal as MOD Administrator.
  2. Navigated to Information Governance → Retention Policies.
  3. Created a new retention policy targeting Exchange, SharePoint, and OneDrive.
  4. Configured retention duration and actions (retain, delete, or both).
  5. Applied policy to all users and verified deployment status.
  6. Tested policy by creating and deleting sample content.
  7. Reviewed audit logs and policy impact in Compliance Center.

Outcome

Retention policy successfully configured and validated. Microsoft 365 now enforces data lifecycle rules to support compliance, reduce risk, and manage storage efficiently.
