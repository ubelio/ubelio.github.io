HIPAA Compliance Across Microsoft 365 Workloads

Enforce HIPAA minimum necessary restrictions and prevent external sharing of PHI using Microsoft Purview DLP policies.

Home Projects Scripts Home-Lab Config Labs Contact

Tools Used

  • Microsoft Purview Data Loss Prevention (DLP)
  • Microsoft 365 E5
  • Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, OneDrive, Microsoft Teams
  • Sensitive Info Types: SSN, DEA Number, ICD-9/10 Codes, Medical Terms
  • Trainable Classifiers: Healthcare, Insurance Files, Medical Forms

Approach

  1. Created three external blocking policies (SharePoint, Teams, Exchange).
  2. Created one internal minimum necessary policy excluding Care Team group.
  3. Validated blocked attempts for non-Care Team users.
  4. Validated allowed attempts for Care Team users.
  5. Captured notifications, policy tips, and Purview alerts.

Policy 3: HIPAA External Block – Exchange

Policy 4: HIPAA Internal Minimum Necessary

Phase 1 – Users Not in Care Team

Phase 2 – Users Added to Care Team

Outcome

Three external blocking policies successfully prevented PHI from leaving the tenant across SharePoint, Teams, and Exchange. The internal minimum necessary policy enforced HIPAA restrictions by blocking all internal PHI sharing except for Care Team group members. Validation confirmed both blocked and allowed scenarios, with clear user notifications and Purview alerts providing compliance visibility.
